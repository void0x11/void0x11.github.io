Current Research

Decentralized Visual Localization for Swarm Robotics

Developing a lightweight CNN/ViT-based visual perception framework for autonomous swarm robots. The system enables real-time formation control without centralized infrastructure by implementing distributed SLAM and adaptive feature selection on edge hardware (Raspberry Pi 4).

Publications & Presentations

SmartBot Core V1.1: A Modular PCB Platform for Educational Robotics

Conference Presentation • SUU Research Symposium • May 2025

Presented a 4-layer PCB design integrating STM32 control, dual H-bridge motor drivers, and efficient power management for autonomous mobile robots. Emphasized EMI mitigation and educational accessibility.

#PCB Design #Robotics

Low-Power SDR Radar for Automotive Applications

Technical Presentation • Industry Challenge Lab • March 2025

Demonstrated FMCW radar signal processing pipeline for range-Doppler analysis. Covered chirp generation, FFT-based processing, and CFAR detection algorithms.
