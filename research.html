Ongoing Research

● In Progress 2025 - Present

Decentralized Visual Localization for Swarm Robotics

Developing a distributed "Visual Compass" utilizing lightweight CNNs (MobileNetV2/YOLO) on Raspberry Pi 4 edge nodes. The system enables autonomous formation control without external motion capture or centralized servers.

Key Contributions

  • Designed a peer-to-peer relative pose estimation pipeline using visual features.
  • Optimized inference latency to < 100ms on generic ARM CPUs.
  • Integrated with ROS2 DDS for robust inter-agent communication.
ROS2 Computer Vision Edge AI
● Prototyping Complete 2024 - 2025

SmartBot Core V1.1: Modular Mixed-Signal Architecture

A custom 4-layer PCB platform designed to replace commercial motor drivers for educational robotics. Features integrated current sensing, high-efficiency buck-boost power stages, and EMI-optimized layout for noise-sensitive sensors.

Key Contributions

  • Engineered mixed-signal stackup achieving -40dB noise reduction on analog rails.
  • Implemented active current limiting and reverse-polarity protection.
Altium Designer STM32 Power Electronics
