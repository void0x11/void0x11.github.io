I am a researcher and engineer working at the intersection of low-level hardware constraints and intelligent algorithms.

My current work investigates decentralized architectures for swarm robotics and the optimization of deep learning models for resource-constrained edge devices. I am passionate about building systems that are not only theoretically sound but also robust and deployable in the real world.
