About

I am a research scholar working at the intersection of low-level hardware constraints and intelligent algorithms.

My research investigates decentralized coordination mechanisms for multi-robot systems and the optimization of deep learning architectures for resource-constrained edge devices. I am committed to developing systems that are theoretically rigorous, empirically validated, and deployable in real-world environments.
